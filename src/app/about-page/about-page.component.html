ABOUT US

Our Team worked on following MNC’s , each one having more than 10+ years of experience IBM , INFOSYS , CAPGEMINI , Cognizant , United Health Group , SISL (INDIA) , SIEMENS (USA) , Apollo Health Street, CARE ,IDEA OBJECT, TELSTRA , CA Technologies , Motorola, MACH , Lycos , HP (Hewlett-Packard )

OUR VISION

Our vision is to create an environment where candidates actively foster the freedom to productively and enthusiastically pursue and enhance their skill set which would add value to their employers. Inspire all individuals at our campus to pursue lifelong learning through a dedication to intellectual and personal growth by facilitating a respectful and inclusive environment with regard to individual, societal, and global perspectives.

OUR MISSION

Second Campus Learning Solutions is a student-centered, community-minded, partnership-oriented training campus that offers a full gamut of programs to enhance the educational, economic, and cultural environment for candidates who are in quest of IT careers. To serve students from diverse backgrounds and offer the highest quality instruction designed to develop critical thinking, creative, quantitative, leadership and communication skills of students. Our faculty, staff, and administrators empower individuals with the freedom to learn, grow, and develop as leaders.
